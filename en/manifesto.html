L.I.S.I. Manifesto

L.I.S.I. is an open, interdisciplinary, and international laboratory dedicated to research, development, and innovation in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Digital Ethics, and Scientific Education.

Our purpose is to promote ethical and socially impactful technological solutions by integrating science, technology, and governance for a cognitive and collaborative future.

We act as a catalyst for strategic projects, talent development, and international articulation, aiming to transform global challenges into sustainable development opportunities.

The laboratory fosters collaboration among researchers, developers, educators, and institutions, promoting the training of specialized professionals and the creation of a globally connected ecosystem.

L.I.S.I. operates through a distributed network of collaborators, virtual laboratories, international partnerships, and cutting-edge digital infrastructure, with a focus on transparency, sustainability, and open innovation.

Furthermore, the laboratory promotes events, courses, and publications to disseminate knowledge and encourage the adoption of responsible practices in technology.
